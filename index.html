Meet participant NM0004S5.

In 2015, researchers at the University of Oslo tasked participants, including NM0004S5, to stand completely still while listening to different sound stimuli. The objective was to investigate the effects of music stimuli on movement, mainly how the stimuli would induce involuntary motion even when participants tried to remain perfectly still. Participant motion was tracked by a high-precision camera system in millimeter resolution.
