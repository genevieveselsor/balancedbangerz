About the Data

Participants were asked to stand as still as possible and presented with a series of auditory stimuli. Each participant was equipped with a head marker to track their head movement over a continous 6 minutes, listening to 4 different stimuli in a randomized order. The stimuli included 60 seconds excerpts of silence, salsa, meditation, and electronic dance music (EDM). Note that there silence was included as a stimulus 3 times. The data was collected from 12 groups, each with 3-12 participants.

Limitations to the data: We do not have any rotational data, because there was only one head marker on each participant.

Note on visualizations: We included only groups with 12 participants. We visualize the first block of silence in each group's sequence.

Groups were given stimuli in the order as follows:

  5. EDM
